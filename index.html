Rich Dad Poor Dad

Robert

The story begins with the author as a young boy, observing the contrasting financial mindsets and behaviors of his own father, the "poor dad," and a friend's father, the "rich dad." The poor dad held a high position in education and emphasized the importance of academic success, job security, and living within one's means. The rich dad, a successful entrepreneur, believed in building assets, investing wisely, and acquiring financial knowledge.
